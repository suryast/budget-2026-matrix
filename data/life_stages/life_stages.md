## Early-career accumulator (20s)

Building first portfolio; little CGT history; may rent; pre-FHB.

## Mid-career builder (30s)

Often first-home buyer or recent buyer; growing portfolio; possibly young family.

## Peak-earner (40s)

Top-bracket income common; portfolio compounding; super contributions material.

## Pre-retiree, bridge phase (50s)

Building non-super assets to bridge to preservation age; CGT timing matters.

## Retiree, decumulation (60+)

Drawing down; pension-phase super possible; lower marginal-rate windows matter.
